Software Developer

Arnav Patel

I'm a computer science student at Georgia Tech focused on building and shipping real world products. Currently, I'm a founding engineer at TapTab, a restaurant discovery platform. I lead frontend development across mobile and web using React Native and extend beyond interfacts to and Express + PostgreSQL backend. Before that, I intererned at Hulu on the video streaming team, extensively contributing to their backend systems using SpringBoot. Outside of work, I spend time on personal projects spanning AI agents and full-stack apps. I enjoy building these rpojects from the ground up and taking my rough ideas to fully fledged systems. The goal is for one of them to become a company. If you're a founder, engineer, or builder working on something ambitious, I'd love to connect!
